Why Genesis exists

Kenya's fertility gap needs a Kenyan answer.

Between 10 and 15 percent of Kenyan couples face infertility. Until now, the fertility industry has relied on imported donor samples built around Western genetic profiles — missing the conditions most prevalent in East Africa.

Genesis was built to change that. Our screening panel was developed specifically for Kenya's genetic landscape. We are the first programme in East Africa built under the ART Bill 2022.

And we built it around the donor. Every step is designed to be dignified, private, and genuinely good for your health.

Eligibility criteria

Are you the right fit?

We list our criteria openly because your time matters.

You qualify if…
  • Aged 18 to 35
  • Resident in Kenya or can visit regularly
  • BMI 18.5–27.5
  • Non-smoker or stopped 6+ months ago
  • No recreational drugs
  • Can commit 7–9 months
  • Willing to provide family history
  • Comfortable with anonymity & family limit
Automatic disqualifiers…
  • HIV, Hepatitis B/C, active STIs
  • Sickle cell disease
  • Certain hereditary conditions
  • Concurrent donor programme participation

What we screen for

The most thorough panel in East Africa.

Built for Kenya's genetic landscape, not a Western checklist.

Semen quality

Count, motility, morphology vs WHO thresholds. Cryosurvival post-freeze.

Infectious disease

HIV I & II, Hepatitis B & C, syphilis, gonorrhoea, chlamydia, CMV, HTLV I & II.

East Africa genetics

Sickle cell, thalassaemia, G6PD, karyotype — local genomic data.

Expanded carrier

Cystic fibrosis, SMA, fragile X, hereditary hearing loss, cancer risk.

Physical exam

Full physical by medical director. BMI, cardiovascular baseline, urological.

Psychological

Motivation, mental health baseline, long-term implications. Licensed psychologist.

Identity verification

National ID, criminal history, educational background confirmation.

Ongoing monitoring

Quarterly health checks throughout. 10-year follow-up for hereditary conditions.
